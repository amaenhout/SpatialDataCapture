SBS Team

The Stories Behind Streets project is our end-product for the Spatial Data Capture, Storage and Analysis module by the Bartlett Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA). Through the delivered lectures and practicals, the essential skills in data storage and classification, data mining and analysis, and data visualisation were acquired.

This project was made possible through the collaboration of five students passionate about cities, data and culture. Here are the people behind SBS - coming from Singapore, Belgium, and Switzerland!
